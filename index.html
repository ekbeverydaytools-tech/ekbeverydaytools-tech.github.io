How this works

Every tool on this site is a single, self-contained web page. When you open one, your browser downloads the whole thing — the layout, the styling, and the calculation logic — in one request. After that, nothing else is fetched and nothing is sent back. Type an invoice amount, a loan balance, or a list of time zones, and the math happens on your own device using plain JavaScript.

That design has three practical benefits. First, privacy: your figures, client names, and schedules never travel over the network, so there is nothing for us to store, leak, or sell. Second, speed: with no server round-trips, results update the instant you type. Third, reliability: once a page has loaded, it keeps working on a flaky connection, on a plane, or saved to your desktop as a local file.

There is no account system because none is needed — your inputs stay in the page, and a few tools offer an optional save that uses your browser's local storage only. If you want to double-check any result, view the page source: all of the logic is readable, unminified JavaScript.

Frequently asked questions

Are these tools really free?

Yes. All five tools are completely free, with no paywalls, trials, usage limits, or premium tiers. Because everything runs in your browser, serving them costs almost nothing, so there is no reason to charge.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

No. Invoice details, income figures, loan balances, and schedules are processed entirely on your device by JavaScript running in the page. Nothing you type is transmitted, logged, or stored on any server.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes. Each tool is one self-contained file with no external dependencies, so once a page has loaded it keeps working without a connection. You can even save a page to your computer and open it later from a local file.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There are no accounts, logins, or email signups anywhere on the site. Open a tool, use it, and close the tab — that is the entire flow.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. Every page is responsive and works on screens from small phones up to large desktop monitors, in any modern browser, with no app to install.
