How this works

This invoice generator builds a print-ready invoice directly in your browser. Fill in your business details, your client’s information, and your line items; the preview updates with every keystroke. Quantities are multiplied by unit prices, the discount percentage is applied to the subtotal, and tax is then calculated on the discounted amount — the order most bookkeepers expect. Every figure is rounded to two decimal places, and empty fields simply count as zero, so the totals are always valid.

When the invoice looks right, click Print / Save as PDF. Your browser’s print dialog opens, and choosing “Save as PDF” as the destination produces a clean one-page document containing only the invoice itself — the form, this text, and everything else on the page is excluded by the print stylesheet.

Everything you type is stored in your browser’s local storage as you go, so your business details, currency, and tax rate are still there the next time you open the page. To bill your next client, just change the invoice number, dates, and line items. Nothing is ever uploaded — the page makes no network requests at all, which also means it keeps working offline once loaded. Use Clear to wipe the saved data and start from a blank invoice.

Frequently asked questions

How do I save the invoice as a PDF?

Click Print / Save as PDF, then choose “Save as PDF” as the destination in your browser’s print dialog. Only the invoice itself is printed — the form and the rest of the page are excluded automatically.

Is my invoice data private?

Yes. The page runs entirely in your browser and makes no network requests. Your details are saved only to your own browser’s local storage so they are still there next time; nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

How are the discount and tax calculated?

The discount percentage is applied to the subtotal first, then tax is calculated on the discounted amount. All amounts are rounded to two decimal places, and empty or invalid numbers are treated as zero.

Can I create my next invoice faster?

Yes. Your business details, currency, tax rate, and notes are remembered automatically between visits. Just update the invoice number, dates, and line items for the next client and print again.

Which currencies are supported?

USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), CAD (CA$), and AUD (A$). The symbol you pick is used throughout the invoice. Amounts are not converted between currencies — no data leaves your browser.
