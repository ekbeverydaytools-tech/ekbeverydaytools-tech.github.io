Frequently asked questions

How fast is the turnaround, really?

Starter tools are delivered within 24 hours of the upfront payment. Pro tools take 24–48 hours depending on complexity, and White-label packages take up to 72 hours. If something about your request would take longer, you'll hear that before any money changes hands — not after.

What counts as one tool?

One tool is one single-page utility that does one job: a calculator, a converter, a generator, a planner. A mortgage calculator is one tool; a mortgage calculator plus a separate rent-vs-buy comparison is two. If you're not sure how your idea splits up, describe it in your email and you'll get an honest count back with a quote.

How does payment work?

You receive an invoice for half the tier price upfront, and the remaining half is invoiced when the finished tool is delivered. No subscriptions, no hidden fees — two invoices and you're done.

What if I don't like the result?

Every tier includes revision rounds: one for Starter, two for Pro, and unlimited light revisions for 14 days on White-label. You review the delivered tool, send your change requests, and only pay the remaining balance once you've approved the final version.

Can the tool connect to my existing website?

Yes. Because the tool is a single self-contained file, you can upload it to any host, link to it from your navigation, or embed it in an existing page with an iframe — it will match your look if you choose the White-label tier. Tools run entirely in the browser, so connecting to your database or backend is outside the standard tiers, but you can ask for a custom quote.
